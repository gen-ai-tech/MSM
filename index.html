Our Process

Refined Through Precision Science

At Maharaja MSM, every grain undergoes rigorous multi-stage refinement — from solar-evaporated brine to food-safe, chemically pure finished product.

01

Solar Evaporation & Harvesting

Seawater channeled into evaporation pans, solar concentration, peak salinity harvest.

02

Washing & Centrifugal Purification

Removal of impurities, surface moisture, magnesium compounds.

03

Drying & Moisture Control

Rotary dryers reduce moisture to <0.1%, free-flowing crystals.

04

Iodization & Anti-Caking

Potassium iodate per FSSAI norms, anti-caking for humid environments.
